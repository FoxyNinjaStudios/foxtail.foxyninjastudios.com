Platform Agnostic

Foxtail works on any iOS or Android device with AR support. Stream position and orientation data in real-time to your favorite apps. Completely app agnostic, creating seamless integration with existing workflows. No special hardware. No headset. Just you, your phone, and the space around you.

Plug and Play

Foxtail is unintrusive and easy to set up. No special hardware or software is required. Just install the app and start streaming position data in real-time. Move through the space. Shape the sound. Influence the visuals. The sky is the limit as physical presence becomes part of the show.

Real time

Foxtail streams position and orientation data in real-time over a WebSocket connection. The latency is typically less than 10 milliseconds, with 30fps (up to 60fps on supported devices) ensuring smooth and responsive tracking in real-world applications.
